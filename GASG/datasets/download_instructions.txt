=====================================
 Dataset Download Instructions
=====================================

1. KITTI Raw Data (for Eigen split evaluation)
   URL: http://www.cvlibs.net/datasets/kitti/raw_data.php
   Download the following sequences used in the Eigen test split.
   Extract to: data/kitti/raw/

   For pre-computed GT depth maps:
   Download from: https://github.com/nianticlabs/monodepth2
   Extract to: data/kitti/gt_depths/

   Eigen test split file list:
   Download from: https://github.com/nianticlabs/monodepth2/blob/master/splits/eigen/test_files.txt
   Place at: splits/eigen_test_files.txt

2. PersonDataset (our synthetic dense-crowd benchmark)
   URL: https://huggingface.co/datasets/jibingyang111/PersonDataset
   Extract to: data/PersonDataset/

   Expected structure after extraction:
     data/PersonDataset/
       train/
         left/   *.png
         right/  *.png
         depth/  *.png  (or depth_left_truth/ from HuggingFace)
       test/
         left/   *.png
         right/  *.png
         depth/  *.png  (or depth_left_truth/ from HuggingFace)

3. KITTI Stereo 2015 (for GASG training on KITTI)
   URL: http://www.cvlibs.net/datasets/kitti/eval_scene_flow.php?benchmark=stereo
   Download: "data_scene_flow.zip"
   Extract to: data/kitti/stereo_2015/

=====================================
 After downloading, your data/ directory should look like:

 data/
 ├── kitti/
 │   ├── raw/           (KITTI raw sequences)
 │   ├── gt_depths/     (pre-computed GT depths for Eigen split)
 │   └── stereo_2015/   (KITTI Stereo 2015 training data)
 └── PersonDataset/
     ├── train/
     │   ├── left/
     │   ├── right/
     │   └── depth/
     └── test/
         ├── left/
         ├── right/
         └── depth/
=====================================
